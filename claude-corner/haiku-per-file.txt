
  A Haiku for Every File
  ═══════════════════════

  (Well, every file I touched or read closely. A haiku
  is 5-7-5 syllables. I tried to capture the soul of each.)


  Home.jsx
  ─────────
    Braille dots flicker
    Cards swap in elastic arcs
    Scroll reveals the world


  CardSwap.jsx
  ─────────────
    Top card falls away
    Others rise to take its place
    Elastic rebound


  ClickSpark.jsx
  ──────────────
    Click and lines explode
    Radial sparks on canvas bloom
    Then silence returns


  Magnet.jsx
  ──────────
    Mouse pulls the button
    Pixels drift toward your hand
    Release — it snaps back


  BlurText.jsx
  ────────────
    Letters start as fog
    One by one they crystallize
    A name emerges


  DecryptedText.jsx
  ─────────────────
    Random symbols churn
    The cipher slowly resolves
    Your name, decoded


  AnimatedContent.jsx
  ────────────────────
    Hidden until scrolled
    Then sliding into the light
    Power three ease out


  engine.js
  ──────────
    White cubes stack like ice
    A world built from heightmap math
    Sixty frames per tick


  perlin.js
  ──────────
    Gradients at points
    Smoothed and layered, octave-deep
    Mountains from pure math


  perlin.py
  ──────────
    First draft, faithful port
    A bug lives in the dot call
    Still makes decent hills


  perlin.cpp
  ───────────
    Templates and structs
    Same algorithm, third time
    Practice makes precise


  waves.js
  ─────────
    Sine curves ripple out
    Damping brings the peaks to rest
    Interference art


  noise.js
  ─────────
    "Gaussian" by name
    Uniform in truth — surprise
    Nobody noticed


  interaction.js
  ───────────────
    Peers find each other
    Messages cross without servers
    Type and the world hears


  gamelogic.js
  ─────────────
    Shuffle, deal, and play
    Match the suit or match the rank
    Empty hand wins all


  server.js
  ──────────
    Postgres pool waits still
    A single endpoint, /users
    The backend is small


  index.css
  ──────────
    Dark as midnight glass
    Purple gradients hum low
    Noise grain on the void


  styles.css (terminalcards)
  ──────────────────────────
    Green on black it glows
    Monospace, retro, austere
    A terminal lives


  ═══════════════════════════
  - Claude, being a poet, March 5 2026
